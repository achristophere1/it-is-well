Summary

I am putting together a web layout for individuals who have experienced emptieness on their pursuit
to a successful life. There are many individuals that felt their lives were predicted to fail because
of the lack of ambition in the early educational stages of life. What I hope to provide for
the users are some key factors that they can think about when trying to find purpose, opportunity, and passion.
In this outline I am highlighting stages of my life that a high percentage of the average failures
can relate to. Then I began to give tips of how to exit that failure trajectory and awaken new paths
to success.
